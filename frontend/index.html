Language model required

ALDA requires a language model throughout the search process:

  • Parse your research question — extract keywords, date ranges, and Boolean queries
  • Translate queries — search regional databases in their native language
  • Score results for relevance — so the most useful sources appear first

You need an API key from a language model provider. Setup takes about 2 minutes. Your key is stored locally in your browser.

Add your own sources

Upload a CSV, JSON, or database file. ALDA merges them with your search results and removes duplicates automatically.

Drag & drop your file here, or click to select

Accepted: CSV, JSON, JSONL, DuckDB

How to prepare a CSV file

Each row is one source. You need at least one of: a URL or a DOI (e.g. 10.1234/example).

Optional columns:

  • title
  • authors — semicolon-separated, e.g. Smith J; Jones A
  • year — e.g. 2022
  • abstract
  • venue — journal name, conference, or publisher
url,title,authors,year
https://example.org/paper1,My Paper Title,Smith J; Jones A,2022
